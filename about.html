About Virtual Medical Missions

Information about transforming healthcare through innovation and compassion.

Vision

We envision countries internationally, where all individuals in need, regardless of their background, are able to have equal access to quality healthcare. We seek to address the geographical barriers that stop rural and remote communities from getting the healthcare they need.

Mission

Our mission is to improve healthcare access and outcomes for underdeveloped countries by providing affordable high-quality telemedicine services. Our aim is to bridge the gap between patients and healthcare access, within these countries.

"At Virtual Medical Missions, our heart beats for the underserved communities of Kenya and beyond. Through the power of telemedicine, we aim to bridge the gap in access to medical care, bringing hope, healing, and a legacy of compassion to those who need it most." - CEO

Background

Established in November of 2021, the poor health services due to inadequate health workers and lack of medical supplies in Kwale County inspired us to begin our journey in paving the way for a healthier future.

Since then, our first clinic has begun operation in Godo, Kenya, as we partner with another non-profit that has built a primary school and church there prior to our clinic opening. By partnering with local community leaders in Godo, a small village inside the Kenyan border with Tanzania, we are able to see patients and increase their quality of life, with quality healthcare.

Starting with Godo, Kenya, we hope to expand our services to other villages in Kenya, and then to the world.

Collaboration

VMM is proud to collaborate with eSTEM and Eastvale Innovators, combining the power of technology, innovation, and community-driven efforts to provide essential healthcare services to underserved communities around the world.
